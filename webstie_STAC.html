SUSTAINABLE TRANSPORTATION ADVOCACY CLUB [STAC].

We are a collection of students at the University of Victoria from a variety of programs who are united around our interest in sustainable transportation projects for our community.

goals

1.To encourage student representation in the conversation around progressive transportation projects.
2.Raise awareness and support for sustainable transportation projects around the CRD.
3.Take action to make our communities better for all road users.
